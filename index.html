why we do what we do what we do

  1. waste little time on overhead

how to dance
 

reach out

we're around. the perfect route would be to go out for a beer and talk. or a attend a talk one of us is doing. but the world is huge, so them email things are fine.

let's do something small

we're not really into working with people we don't know - so a good idea to start would be to set up a tiny workshop or talk (over at your place) where we can get to know eachother - so we can help you out at least a bit, while getting to know you better.

let's build a relationship

if we feel there's a vibe of understanding and common goals - let's go on and build a fantastic relationship.
